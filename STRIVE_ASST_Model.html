How the model works. BTC price compounds at your CAGR. Each active month the SATA ATM raises capital → buys BTC (price ÷ holdings, no common dilution — that's the amplification thesis). Preferred grows with raises (and dividends, if funded by new SATA). Common shares grow only by your dilution input. Share price = (mNAV × BTC NAV − debt − preferred + cash) ÷ shares, with mNAV gliding linearly from today's level to your stabilization target. Amplification ratio = (debt + preferred) ÷ BTC NAV; CEBE (Common Equity Bitcoin Exposure) = holdings × (1 − amplification) ÷ shares. Not investment advice — indicative figures for scenario exploration.
